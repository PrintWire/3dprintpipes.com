Industries

Where 3D Printed Pipe Excels

Oil, Gas & Hydraulics

Pressure-rated 316L manifolds, custom hydraulic block replacements, and subsea fitting assemblies with integrated O-ring grooves and port threads.

Aerospace & Defence

Lightweight titanium and Inconel ducting for propulsion, bleed air, and fuel systems. Topology-optimised brackets and support structures integrated into the pipe assembly.

HVAC & Building Services

Custom duct branches, acoustic baffles, and diffuser geometries for commercial and industrial HVAC. Noise-attenuating splitter forms impossible to fabricate in sheet metal.

Marine & Offshore

Corrosion-resistant pipe fittings and manifolds for seawater systems, ballast, and cooling circuits. Complex branching without welding for reduced leak-point count.
